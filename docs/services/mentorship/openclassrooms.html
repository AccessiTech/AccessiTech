OpenClassrooms Partnership

Through mid-2025, conor served as a mentor for OpenClassrooms students—career-switchers building skills in web development, UX design, and project management. That operational precedent informs how AccessiTech Mentorship works today: accessible education for people building second or third chapters, grounded in real-world project work and accountability to community standards.

Real mentorship looks like accountability to real-world standards, not just content delivery. The OpenClassrooms engagement shaped how AccessiTech Mentorship works today — grounding every learning relationship in practical project work, professional community expectations, and genuine career progression.

What's Included

  • Mentorship for career-switchers in web development, UX design, and project management
  • Real-world project review and feedback cycles
  • Accountability structure aligned to professional community standards
  • Foundation for AccessiTech's current mentorship model and philosophy

Access & Pricing

The OpenClassrooms partnership ran through mid-2025 and is now closed to new students. AccessiTech's current mentorship offerings (CCCs, 1:1 Coaching, SOTC) carry forward the same philosophy — reach out to discuss the best fit for your goals.
